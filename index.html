Move-in / move-out inspection compare

Upload move-in and move-out inspection report PDFs. The tool extracts embedded photos and inspection dates from each report, tags images by area, and compares side-by-side to flag damage that likely occurred during the tenancy. Expected wear and tear is calculated from those dates against standard item lifespans.

This tool is a screening aid, not legal advice or a substitute for a licensed inspection. Always review findings manually and consult qualified counsel before withholding deposit or pursuing charges.
